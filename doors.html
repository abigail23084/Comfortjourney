1 Vinyl A vinyl door refers to a door that consists of a vinyl frame and panel. The frame is typically reinforced with metal or wooden materials to provide additional strength and stability — making the door more robust and reliable. The door panel can be solid or feature glass inserts, allowing natural light to filter through while providing privacy. Cart
2 Glass Sliding glass doors are a type of patio door that slides on a series of rollers located inside a track. In most cases, sliding doors feature two, three, or four panels. They typically open on to a patio, porch, or deck. Cart
3 Wood Wooden doors are functional, display artistry, and work well in different climates. They can be made from solid woods, composites, veneers, or engineered components. Solid wood doors are commonly used for exterior doors due to their strength and security. There are two main categories of wooden doors: interior doors (solid wood, hollow core, solid core) and exterior doors. Solid wood doors are considered a good investment due to their visual appeal, customizability, and environmental benefits. Cart
