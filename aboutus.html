About Us

Having a hard time traveling? Lack the resources? We can help!

As children, the founders of Comfort Journey were obsessed with Chronicles of Narnia and Monsters Inc. They were in awe of the ability to open a door or wardrobe and be in a whole new place. Because of this, they started brainstorming how to incorporate this into the modern world and make travel available to all. Due to air travel being restrictive for pregnant women and certain conditions, the founders were focused on a goal of inclusivity. Working with scientists, government officials, legal representatives, and foreign leaders, Comfort Journey vowed to find the perfect solution for all. Drawing inspiration from the Chronicles of Narnia and Monsters Inc., we founded a company that transports people around the world…all from the comfort of their own homes. By buying custom-made furniture, doors, remotes, etc., our buyers can travel all around the world. Less hassle than airports and car travel. So, grab your passport, suitcase, and join us for the journey of a lifetime.

***Our company values and respects the laws of all destinations, therefore, if you break any of the laws, you will be blipped back to your home and banned from future visits. ***
