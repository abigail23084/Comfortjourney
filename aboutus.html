About Us

Having a hard time traveling? Lack the resources? We can help!

At Comfort Journey, our founding team drew inspiration from two beloved worlds: the enchanting realms of Narnia and the imaginative universe of Monsters, Inc. Just as the Pevensie siblings discovered a magical wardrobe leading to a land of adventure and wonder, we envisioned a way for people to escape into their own extraordinary experiences right from their living rooms. Additionally, the whimsical idea of transporting into alternate realms, as seen in Monsters, Inc. with its imaginative doors, sparked our desire to create teleporting furniture that seamlessly connects everyday spaces with the excitement of the unknown. Our mission is to reimagine comfort and adventure, offering teleporting couches, doors, windows, and wardrobes that transform the ordinary into the extraordinary, allowing everyone to embark on their own journeys of discovery and delight, one teleportation at a time.

***Our company values and respects the laws of all destinations, therefore, if you break any of the laws, you will be blipped back to your home and banned from future visits. ***
